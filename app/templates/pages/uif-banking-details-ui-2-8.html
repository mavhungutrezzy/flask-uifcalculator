UIF Banking Details and UI-2.8 Form Guide

Your UIF payment depends on banking details that can be verified. Incorrect or outdated details can cause a blocked or delayed payment.

What Is the UI-2.8 Form?

The UI-2.8 form is used for UIF banking details. Department of Employment and Labour unemployment and maternity claim guidance lists UI-2.8 among the documents needed for benefit claims.

When Banking Details Cause UIF Delays

  • The bank account is closed or inactive
  • The account holder details do not match your UIF profile
  • Your surname changed and banking records were not updated
  • uFiling shows incorrect banking details and asks for an update
  • The claim status shows Payment Blocked or Payment Reversed
The uFiling training guidance notes that if banking details are incorrect on the system, claimants may need to download and complete the UI-2.8 form to update them.

How to Reduce Banking Verification Problems

Use Your Own Account

Use an account in your name and make sure the name, ID and surname records match.

Check Status Often

If a payment is blocked, act quickly on any UIF request for banking documents.

For related claim status messages, use the UIF status check guide.

This guide is informational. Confirm banking form requirements with UIF or the Department of Employment and Labour.
