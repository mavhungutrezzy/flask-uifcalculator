POPIA Notice

How UIF Calculators approaches personal information under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act.

Last updated: 11 May 2026

What POPIA Is

The Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA) is South Africa's privacy law for the processing of personal information by public and private bodies.

The Act is intended to protect personal information, set minimum requirements for lawful processing, establish the Information Regulator, and give people rights relating to their personal information.

Our Approach

UIF Calculators is designed to provide estimates without asking for unnecessary personal information. The unemployment, leave, VAT, and compound interest calculators use the numbers you enter to produce an estimate on the website.

We do not ask for your ID number, UIF claim number, uFiling password, banking details, employer details, or medical records.

Personal Information We May Process

Depending on how you use the website, limited technical information may be processed through analytics, advertising, security, hosting, and browser technologies.

  • Device, browser, and approximate location information
  • Pages visited and interactions with website features
  • Cookie identifiers used for analytics or advertising
  • Information you voluntarily send if you contact the website owner

Why We Process Information

We process limited information to operate the website, show calculator results, understand site performance, improve content, prevent abuse, and support advertising that keeps the calculators free to use.

We aim to collect only what is reasonably needed for these purposes.

Cookies, Analytics and Advertising

This website may use cookies and similar technologies for analytics and advertising, including services such as Google Analytics and Google AdSense.

You can manage or block cookies in your browser settings. Some features, analytics, or advertisements may not work as expected if cookies are disabled.

Your POPIA Rights

Subject to POPIA and other applicable laws, you may have rights to ask what personal information is held about you, request correction or deletion where appropriate, object to certain processing, and lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator.

If you contact us about your information, we may need enough detail to identify the request and respond appropriately.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the website and reduce unnecessary personal information collection. No online service can guarantee complete security.

Please do not enter sensitive information, passwords, claim credentials, ID numbers, or banking details into calculator fields.

Third-Party Websites

Some pages link to external websites, including government services and third-party resources. Those websites have their own privacy and POPIA practices.

Review the relevant external website's privacy notice before submitting personal information.

Information Regulator

The Information Regulator is the independent South African body established under POPIA. It monitors and enforces compliance with POPIA and PAIA.

You can learn more from the Information Regulator South Africa or read the Protection of Personal Information Act 4 of 2013 on the South African Government website.

Related Policy

For more detail about analytics, advertising, cookies, and external links, please read our Privacy Policy.
