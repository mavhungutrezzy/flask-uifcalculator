Contact

Where to go for calculator questions, UIF claims, and official support.

Website Enquiries

For website feedback, correction requests, or privacy-related questions, use the contact method made available by the UIF Calculators site owner.

Please do not send ID numbers, banking details, passwords, claim numbers, or sensitive documents.

Official UIF Help

UIF Calculators cannot access your UIF profile, claim status, payments, or uFiling account.

For official help, use the Department of Employment and Labour or uFiling website:

  • Department of Employment and Labour
  • uFiling login

Before You Contact UIF

Have your ID, UI-19 form, payslips, banking confirmation, and claim reference ready where applicable. Never share passwords with anyone claiming to help with a UIF claim.
