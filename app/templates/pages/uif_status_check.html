Check UIF Status Online

You can check your UIF status online through the official uFiling portal. The system allows South Africans to:

  • check UIF application status,
  • view UIF payments,
  • check UIF balance,
  • track UIF credits,
  • and monitor claim progress online.

Most UIF claims, payment updates, and credit information are available through the uFiling login dashboard.

How to Check UIF Status Online

Many South Africans search for how to check UIF status online after applying for unemployment, maternity, or illness benefits.

To check your UIF status online:

  1. Visit the official uFiling website
    Go to the UIF uFiling login page.
  2. Log in with your ID
    Use your South African ID number and password. If you don't have an account, register before checking your claim.
  3. Open your claim details
    From the dashboard, choose Benefit Payments, then select View My Claims.
  4. View your UIF status
    Check the latest status, case number, payment updates, and claim notes. For example, Sent to Assessor means your claim is under review.

uFiling Login Status Check

To perform a uFiling login status check:

  1. Visit the official uFiling website
  2. Log in using your South African ID number and password
  3. Open "Benefit Payments"
  4. Select "View My Claims"
  5. View your UIF status, payment updates, and balance

The uFiling portal may display statuses such as:

  • Pending
  • Sent to Assessor
  • Payment Processed
  • Payment Reversed
  • Finalised

UIF Status Check Online & USSD

If you do not have internet access, you can still perform a UIF status check using USSD.

UIF USSD Status Check

Dial:

*134*843#

Then:

  • choose Claims or Payments,
  • enter your details,
  • and view your UIF status or payment information.

This method is commonly used for UIF payment status checks, balance checks, and claim tracking without internet access.

The UIF payment status section shows whether your payments:

Common UIF Payment Statuses

Payment Processed

Your UIF payment has been approved and sent for payment.

Payment Reversed

Bank details may not match your ID or surname.

Payment Blocked

Additional documents or banking verification may be required.

Payment Finalised

The payment cycle or available credit days may be completed.

UIF Application Status Check

After submitting your claim, you can monitor your UIF application status online through uFiling.

The portal may show:

  • application received,
  • pending review,
  • approved,
  • rejected,
  • or payment processing stages.

Checking your UIF status regularly helps you identify issues early, especially missing documents or banking verification problems.

Common UIF Status Meanings

Pending Adjudication

Your claim is still under review.

Sent to Assessor

A UIF assessor is reviewing your documents.

Parked

Outstanding documents may still be needed.

Auto Close

The claim may still require processing or follow-up.

Cancelled

The application or payment request was stopped.

Completed by Assessor

Assessment is complete, but payment may still process separately.

Why Your UIF Status May Not Change

Some UIF claims remain unchanged for weeks because of:

  • banking verification delays,
  • missing employer declarations,
  • high UIF claim volumes,
  • or incomplete supporting documents.

If your UIF status has not changed for a long time, contact UIF or visit your nearest Labour Centre.
