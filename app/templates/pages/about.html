About UIF Calculators

Independent calculators and guides for South Africans planning around UIF and everyday finance questions.

What This Site Does

UIF Calculators provides free tools that help estimate UIF unemployment benefits, leave-related UIF top-ups, VAT amounts, and compound interest outcomes.

The goal is to make common calculations easier to understand before you use official services or seek professional advice.

Independent Website

UIF Calculators is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by the Department of Employment and Labour, UIF, uFiling, SARS, or any South African government department.

How to Use the Results

Use calculator results as planning estimates only. Official claims, payments, and approvals are handled by the relevant official bodies.

For details on formulas and assumptions, read our Methodology page.
